üìñ How to Play

  • Create an account - Log in with your Google account to track your progress and earn points.
  • Choose a puzzle - Select an active puzzle from the list to begin solving.
  • Read carefully - Each puzzle has a description and possibly artifacts (images, text, data) that contain clues.
  • Use hints wisely - Hints are released over time. Each hint halves your potential points!
  • Submit your answer - When you think you've found the solution, submit it. You can try as many times as you want.
  • Earn points - Correct answers earn you points. The earlier you solve (with fewer hints), the more points you get!
  • Climb the leaderboard - Compete with other solvers for the top spots.
