✦ THE MYSTERY OF MAGIC SQUARES ✦

A magic square is a square grid of numbers where every row, every column, and both long diagonals all add up to the exact same total.

People have studied magic squares for hundreds of years. They appear in old puzzles, art, and even stories about magic and hidden knowledge.

Example: A 3×3 Magic Square

In this famous 3×3 magic square, every row, column, and diagonal adds up to 15.

8 1 6
3 5 7
4 9 2
15
